Public Distribution System (PDS)

The Public Distribution System (PDS) is a national program in India aimed at providing essential commodities like food grains, sugar, and kerosene at subsidized rates to eligible beneficiaries. It ensures equitable distribution and monitors ration distribution to prevent leakage and misuse.

Our AI-enabled monitoring system detects suspicious shops and beneficiaries, predicts future anomalies, calculates risk scores, and helps authorities maintain transparency and efficiency in the PDS.
